FAQ

What payment methods do you accept?

Answer: Cash on delivery, debit card and online transfer.


How many can I buy?

Answer: As many as you want! at Tart-Licious we brings you tart sets that is perfect for any occasion and perfect for sharing with your friends, families and colleagues.


What are the uniqueness of TART-LICIOUS?

Answer: TART-LICIOUS offer a variety of unique flavours to our customers but we will also sell the tart base to our customers so that they will have their own freedom to experiment with new flavors on their own and also decorate their own tarts based on what they like. Plus, we also offer a delivery service parallel with the MCO orders.


What are the ingredients of the TART-LICIOUS?

Answer: Our original tarts contain cheese, milk, flour, sugar and egg.


What is the best way to eat the tarts?

Answer: Our cheese tarts can be eaten chilled, re-heated or fresh. We always recommend them to be eaten fresh from our ovens!


Are TART-LICIOUS halal certified?

Answer: Our tarts and soft can be consumed by everyone because the ingredients we will use are all halal, and it is our main priority to make our tarts to be enjoyed by all age and ethnicity groups.
